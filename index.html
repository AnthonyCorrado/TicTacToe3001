TicTacToe 3001

How to play:

The board consists of 24 playable squares, with only nine active at a time. The grid shifts throughout the match changing the active field of play.

Players alternate turns placing their marker in an attempt to score 3-in-a-row. The game does not end on a score. A point is awarded and the match continues. Points can be score regardless of whether all three squares are active at the time.

The match has two waves, with each round requiring a different set of skills. Wave 1 is 30 seconds long with the board shifting from left to right every 5 seconds. Wave 2 is 35 seconds long. After the first five seconds the board will shift every three seconds. The board now moves down and back requiring players to plan ahead.
